Facilities Audit

A facility audit is an audit conducted to identify the current condition of the facility’s assets and establish baseline information of the components, policies and procedures of a new or existing facility’s. While energy audit specifically focused on Energy Conservation Measures (ECM).

Our Approaches

A facility audit is much more complex and cover most of the facility assets. The facility audit normally plans in the facility maintenance master plan at a specific period.

As for UTW, the facility audit was conducted not only according to the master plan but conducted once awarded the contract and before fully taking over the site.
