Vibration Analysis (VBA)



Vibration Analysis (VBA) or monitoring is collected vibrations detected from equipment (asset) and measures them against known failure vibrations to identify potential failure points. VBA help maintenance staff proactively address performance issues before the machines (asset) break on the job.





UTW VBA Job Scopes

Compared with Infrared thermography, the VBA is one of the options used as a Predictive Maintenance tool to measure the asset conditional based on the operational rotating types of machinery and moving structures including:

  • Rotating Shafts
  • Meshing Gear-Teeth
  • Rolling Bearing Elements
  • Rotating Electric Field
  • Fluid Flows
